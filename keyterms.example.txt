# the file is re-read on each /config request.

# Keyterms bias DG's word choice toward a specific SPELLING when the
# acoustics are ambiguous. They do NOT teach pronunciation — if you
# want DG to hear "GELLO" as "jell-o", adding "GELLO" here just makes
# that spelling win when DG hears a similar phoneme.
#
